Overview

Reclean is your kit for creating the cleanest landing page for a cleaning company, complete with design files in Figma! With this amazing package, you'll have all the tools you need to create a top-notch landing page that looks professional and awesome.

Boxed Water offers a large selection of screens and a fully customizable landing page template, allowing you to create a page just right for you in just a few clicks. Say goodbye to dusty templates stored on a shelf for just one project! Thanks to the screen library, you can easily customize a template to suit multiple projects and meet all of your client's needs.

But that's not all - we've gone above and beyond by providing components, color palettes, fonts, and everything else you need to make your job as a developer easier. We understand the value of your time, so we've made sure that you can easily find and customize certain interface elements.

We truly believe that Reclean will help you create a landing page that is both beautiful and effective. We'd love to hear your thoughts and feedback, so please feel free to contact us at unityle.team@gmail.com. We're always ready to listen and support you!
